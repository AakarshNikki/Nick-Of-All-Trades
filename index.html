Developer Developer

Hi I am Aakarsh

My trajectory in software development is defined by a deep-seated curiosity that predates the modern smartphone. From the early days of Java ME and the technical challenge of porting N-Gage titles to standard Nokia handsets, I have spent over a decade evolving alongside the mobile and gaming industries.

The Technical Evolution

I spent years refining my craft within the Android ecosystem (Jellybean through KitKat) before transitioning into high-level game architecture. While I built a deep mastery of Unity (C#) and Unreal Engine (C++), my professional focus underwent a significant shift following the Unity-ironSource merger. Disillusioned by the industry's pivot toward aggressive monetization over developer-centric stability, I made the strategic and ethical choice to transition my primary development to the Godot Engine, advocating for the transparency and resilience of open-source tools.

Today, my work is driven by the conviction that technology must serve the public good. I bridge the gap between complex logic and human impact through three core pillars:

Educational Innovation: I am currently gamifying high school science concepts, transforming rigid curricula into immersive, interactive learning environments.

Narrative Design: Utilizing my background in creative writing and poetry to build games where the story is as robust as the codebase.

AI & Tech Ethics: I serve as an active voice for the ethical use of Artificial Intelligence. I advocate for transparency, data privacy, and the protection of human creativity against the unchecked expansion of automated systems.

I believe that as developers, we are the architects of the digital future, and it is our responsibility to ensure that future is built on ethical foundations.

As A Consultant

I empower startups and small enterprises to scale effectively by replacing traditional, high-cost marketing and advertising models with strategic and ethical AI integration.

In an era of rising digital costs and data vulnerability, I specialize in implementing Privacy-First, Offline AI solutions. My approach ensures that businesses can automate their growth and marketing workflows at a fraction of the usual cost, without ever compromising their most valuable asset: customer data.

  • Efficiency through Local AI: I guide companies in deploying local, open-source Large Language Models (LLMs) and tools that run on existing hardware. This eliminates expensive monthly subscriptions and reduces dependence on third-party advertising platforms.

  • Data Sovereignty & Privacy: By keeping AI operations offline, I ensure that sensitive customer information stays within the company’s walls. This is not just a security measure; it is a commitment to the ethical use of technology.

  • Marketing Integration: I help startups build sustainable brand presence using AI-driven content and strategy that respects the user, avoiding the invasive tracking typical of modern digital marketing.
